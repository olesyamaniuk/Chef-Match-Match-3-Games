How to Play

  • Match Tiles:

    Drag to swap and align three or more identical tiles to clear them and complete level goals.

  • Power Up:

    Combine four or more tiles to create powerful boosters like fish rockets, milk bombs, and rainbow catnip.

  • Complete Objectives:

    Each level has unique objectives, such as collecting treats or freeing trapped kittens, which add variety to your gameplay.

  • Unlock Cats:

    Complete levels to unlock adorable cats with special powers, such as clearing rows or exploding tiles.

  • Explore Levels:

    Progress through increasingly challenging levels and discover new feline-themed environments.

  • Participate in Events:

    Join seasonal events and challenges to earn rare items, bonuses, and exclusive cats!
