How to Play

  • Match Tiles:

    Swap and align three or more identical tiles to clear them and complete cooking challenges.

  • Create Power-Ups:

    Match four or more tiles to unlock powerful boosters like blender blasts, rolling pin combos, and rainbow whisks.

  • Complete Objectives:

    Fulfill level goals like gathering ingredients, plating dishes, or serving customers to progress.

  • Unlock Chefs:

    Meet skilled chefs, each with unique abilities to help you overcome tricky levels.

  • Explore Levels:

    Travel through culinary-themed stages, from homey kitchens to extravagant gourmet restaurants.

  • Participate in Events:

    Join special events to earn rare recipes, exclusive chefs, and unique kitchen upgrades.
